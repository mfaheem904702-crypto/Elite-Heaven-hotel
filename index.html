Our Story

A Legacy of Extraordinary Hospitality

Founded in 1985, Elite Haven Hotels was born from a singular vision: to create spaces where luxury is not merely experienced, but felt in every breath. Our five iconic properties across America stand as monuments to craftsmanship, discretion, and world-class service.

From the golden boulevards of Beverly Hills to the volcanic shores of Maui, each Elite Haven property is meticulously tailored to its surroundings — yet unmistakably unified by our unwavering commitment to excellence.
